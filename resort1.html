The 5-star Asia Gardens Hotel & Thai Spa, a Royal Hideaway Hotel is a luxury hotel with spa and fantastic views of the Mediterranean, perfect for family or couples holiday in the Sierra Cortina mountains. It is 10-15 min. away from Altea and the Terra Mítica and Terra Natura theme parks, and 30 min. from Alicante airport. The 309 spacious rooms are surrounded by exotic gardens, with more than 150,000 plants and Asian themed pool areas. All are decorated in the Balinese style, with fine hardwood features and a unique personality. They are meticulously designed down to the last detail to offer a stay in complete luxury, ensuring maximum well-being. The hotel has 9 themed restaurants where Mediterranean and international Asian cuisine merge. The Thai Spa is a new concept among 5-star spa hotels, providing spiritual and physical well-being with every Thai treatment. The hotel is an essential destination for golf enthusiasts, with courses very close to the hotel, and for families, thanks to the excellent kid’s club, the Asia Gardens Hotel & Thai Spa, a Royal Hideaway Hotel hotel is disabled guest friendly.
